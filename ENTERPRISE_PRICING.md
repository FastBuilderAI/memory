# FastMemory & BuildRight: The Revenue-Based Licensing Model

This document outlines the official licensing governance for the FastMemory engine and the BuildRight engineering layer.

## 🛡️ BuildRight: The Horizontal Layer of Truth
**Status**: 100% Open Source & Free
**License**: MIT
**Target**: All Developers, Teams, and Enterprises

BuildRight is a community-driven ontological layer. Access to the 131+ frameworks, the `generate.py` orchestrator, and the core Topology logic is provided free of charge to ensure the "Horizontal Layer of Truth" is accessible to every engineer on the planet.

---

## 🧠 FastMemory: The Cognitive Engine
**Status**: Community & Enterprise Licensed (Revenue-Based)
**Target**: High-Scale Graph Processing & Production Deployments

The underlying FastMemory engine, which powers the high-speed Louvain clustering and deterministic logic retrieval of BuildRight, is licensed based on your organization's annual revenue:

### 1. FastMemory Community Edition
**Price**: $0 / Forever
**Eligibility**: 
- Individual developers.
- Open Source projects.
- Enterprises with **Annual Revenue < $20,000,000**.
- Educational institutions and researchers.

### 2. FastMemory Enterprise Edition
**Price**: Custom Quote (Volume/Revenue Scaled)
**Eligibility**: 
- Enterprises with **Annual Revenue > $20,000,000**.
- Organizations requiring **Air-Gapped Sync**, high-availability cluster mesh, or SOC2/HIPAA certified distributions.
- Access to **Strategic Architecture Support** for custom legacy ontology mapping.
